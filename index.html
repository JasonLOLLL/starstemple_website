    About Jason

    Hi. My name is Jason Li and I am from Philadelphia. I take great interest in STEM especially in the area of technology. This is my first attempt at building a website and I appreciate the amount of customization. During my middle school days, I got my first PC with little experience in the technologies. I enjoyed playing video games with my friends which brought me into video editing and graphic design for YouTube. I feel like these hobbies are great for me and I would like to keep expanding my field further into STEM. This is the reason that I am transferring for my junior year in high school; I want to be able to reach new opportunities and be the best that I can. I love what I do so far and I cannot wait for more.

    Coding

    I used to make ROBLOX games and I enjoyed the idea of coding. I am trying to learn new languages this year like the ones I'm using right now: HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I also want to learn more about Python and Java. I find it really cool to be able to create amazing applications using my keyboard.

    Content Creation

    I really enjoy creating content such as videos on YouTube and graphic design for profile pictures or banners. This includes editing videos when I get the free time. I record myself and friends playing video games and then edit it down to make an entertaining and funny video for all to enjoy. I find it very fascinating how much the powers of technology can achieve.

    Piano

    I love music. It is such a charm to listen to no matter the mood. To display my affection, I started playing the piano almost two years ago; it is my favorite instrument. I enjoy playing everything ranging from classical music to pop to jazz. Even though I am not that experienced, I wish to continue my piano journey so that one day I can play my favorite piece, Liebesleid (Love's Sorrow).
    inspired by Your Lie in April :)

    About Spencer

    Hello hello, my name is Spencer and I go to Carver High school for Engineering and Science. I thought that this school was a perfect choice for me because it as a lot of STEM classes which I am always interested in. Because of this school, I am able to partake in events like this one. Surprisingly, school is one of the things that I enjoy. While I don't enjoy every class, school keeps me from getting bored in the middle of the day. I like to do many things in my free time but my favorite things I like to do are video editing, cubing, and gaming. These three things have made me who I am today.

    Video Editing

    I like video editing because I always liked seeing a clean final product made from the bare clips provided. For the whole 8 months that I have had my pc, I had premiere pro and after effects installed on my computer. I always found therapy in my creations.

    Speedcubing

    When I was in 7th grade my friend Jason showed me the Rubik's cube. While I had one at home, I never played with it. After seeing how swiftly his fingers rotated each layer of the cube, I was intrigued and I had to get to where he was. This led me to learn how to solve the cube. In one week I was down to 30 seconds. And now I am down to 20. While I am far from being the fastest in the world, I still like cubing to pass the time.

    Keyboards

    I like to customized keyboards for the same reason I like to edit videos. When it comes to building a keyboard, I am given a few parts like the pcb, the case, the plate mount, and the switches and I have to make a keyboard out of them. While I only have one keyboard right now, I am looking into investing lots of money into this hobby Unfortunately not all keyboards will survive. My most recently built keyboard died due to a poor soldering job.
